TANZANIA'S OUTDOOR SPECIALISTS

Founded in 2021, Hiker Store TZ specializes in camping, hiking, and tactical gear designed for durability, comfort, and reliability in outdoor environments.

With locations in Dar es Salaam and Arusha, we proudly serve customers across Tanzania who love adventure, travel, and outdoor exploration.

We support campers, hikers, tour operators, travelers, and field professionals by providing practical gear that makes outdoor experiences safer and more enjoyable. Our mission is to equip people with the right tools to explore nature with confidence.
